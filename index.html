The Energy Club

2900 S Quincy St, Arlington, VA 22206

Located on South Quincy Street in Shirlington, The Energy Club,703-824-0600 , has easy access from local bus routes, Capital Bikeshare, and I-395, and is walking distance from Fairlington, Parkfairfax, and Shirlington Village.

Our Trainers specialize in the following:

  • Sports and Athletic Performance
  • Strength and Condition
  • Corrective Exercise
  • Russian Kettlebell
  • Functional Movement
  • Nutrition & Exercise Recovery
  • Cycling
  • Flexibility and mobility
  • Weight Management
  • TRX
  • Olympic Weightlifting
  • Powerlifting
  • Post rehabilitation
  • And more!
